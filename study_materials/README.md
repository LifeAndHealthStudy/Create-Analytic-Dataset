# Directory Overview

## CONTENT DESCRIPTIONS

-   L+H_Participant_Resource_List.pdf

    -   List of resources given to study participants that could potentially help them address discrimination in relation to race/ethnicity, sex/gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, age, and weight; psychological well-being; and sleep disturbances.

-   L+H_Participant_Screener.pdf

    -   Screening instrument used to identify participants eligible for study enrollment:

        -   Patient at one of the three community health centers (Fenway Health, Harvard Street Neighborhood Health Center, or theMattapan Community Health Center)

        -   25 - 64 years old

        -   Born in the U.S.

        -   Visited the health center within the past 2 years

-   L+H_Qualtrics_Survey_Instrument.pdf

    -   Survey instrument used to collect measures of sociodemographics, self-reported unfair treatment & adverse life events, physical health, and social & civic engagement.
